Our logo contains of 5 leaves in green colour and a blue dot in the middle. The blue dot in the middle represents the planet Earth and the leaves represent the enviorment that we live in.

The idea of the leaves revoling around the Earth also symbolizes recycling.

The font face we used for the logo is Bebas Neue. We chose this particualar font face because of its readabilty.

The main design principles we applied to create the logo are:

  • 1. Consistency
  • 2. Repetition
  • 3. Balance
  • 4. Emphasiss
